Q: What is LanguageExchange ?

Answer
Language-exchange is literally to change their first language. In here, GU Students are supported to look for other helping them with learning their wish-languages. In fact, there are large amount of international students, which means high demand to learn new language.

Q: How to use LanguageExchange ?

Answer
Users are strongly recomended to register Language-exchange. This is becasue the condition of searching people for learning is only registeration. After registeration, the "search" page will be available. in search, users can choose what languages they want to learn. People who have selected national language will be shown up. In the users' information, there are their contact detatil. This contact deatail will be GU_Email.

Q: Why do you use it ?

LanguageExchange could bring a lot of oppoturities for users to get jobs. This is becasue, many people esily move to other country which means, there are high possibilites of working in forrign countries. Thus, Knowing Language is one of powerful benefits.
